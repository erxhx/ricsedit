Privacy Policy.

What we collect

When you book an appointment, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. If your service requires a waiver, we also record your acknowledgement at the time of booking. This information is used solely to manage your appointment and communicate with you about it.

When you visit our website, Google Analytics collects anonymous data — such as pages visited, time on site, and general location — to help us understand how people find and use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

How we use it

Your booking information is used to confirm your appointment, send reminders and follow-ups by email and SMS, and maintain records for the studio. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Services we use

To operate the booking system and send notifications, we work with the following services:

Google Analytics — anonymous website analytics.
Resend — transactional email delivery.
Twilio — SMS notifications.

Each of these services processes data only as necessary to perform its function and is bound by its own privacy policy.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us directly. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
